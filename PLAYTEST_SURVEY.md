# Borderlines Self-Guided Playtest

Please complete this in one sitting (about 20 to 30 minutes).

## Part 1: Action Checklist (Do This First)

Check each item after you complete it.

- [ ] Open the game and land on the home screen.
- [ ] Use the continent filter once.
- [ ] Open Settings once, then close it.
- [ ] Play 3 rounds of Normal Mode.
- [ ] Play 3 rounds of Map Select.
- [ ] Play 3 rounds of Region Chain.
- [ ] In Region Chain, toggle world map off, go to next round, and confirm it stays off.
- [ ] Play 3 rounds of Reverse Border.
- [ ] Play 3 rounds of Battle Mode.
- [ ] Play Competitive Mode if available today.
- [ ] Use Reveal at least once in any mode.
- [ ] In a typing mode, use country suggestions/autocomplete at least once.
